Nsimankhuku

Nsimankhuku is a traditional Malawian dish made with chicken and a variety of vegetables. It is a hearty and flavorful meal that is often enjoyed with rice or nsima (a type of maize porridge). The chicken is typically cooked with onions, tomatoes, and a blend of spices, creating a rich and savory sauce.
Nsimankhuku is a popular dish in Malawi and is often served during special occasions and family gatherings.
It is a delicious way to experience the vibrant flavors of Malawian cuisine.
